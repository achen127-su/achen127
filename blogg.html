Charlie's IT Girls BLOG

Charlie Chen / July 22nd, 2025

Geneieve Grotjan was a revolutionary mathematician that played a large role as a codebreaker for the Army's Signals Intelligence Service (SIS) in 1939, intercepting messages concerning the ongoing world war. She worked to uncode Axis messages, discovering a behavior within the code that allowed the decryption of almost all enemy messaging. In 1946, Genevieve received the Exceptional Civilian Service Award for her great impact as a cryptographer
